## Model Overview

Vidu offers several specialized AI models, each optimized for different types of video generation tasks. Choose the model that best fits your specific use case to achieve optimal results.

### Vidu Turbo
Our standard model optimized for fast video generation while maintaining high quality. Ideal for:
- Rapid prototyping
- Quick content creation
- Applications requiring fast turnaround times

Key characteristics:
- Fast processing speed
- Good quality output
- Cost-effective solution

### Vidu Pro
Our premium model offering the highest quality video generation. Perfect for:
- Professional content creation
- Commercial applications
- Projects requiring the highest fidelity

Key characteristics:
- Superior video quality
- Enhanced detail and realism
- Advanced features and capabilities

### Model Selection Guide
When choosing a model, consider:
1. **Quality Requirements**: For highest quality, choose Vidu Pro. For good quality with faster processing, choose Vidu Turbo.
2. **Budget Considerations**: Vidu Turbo offers a more economical option for high-volume applications.
3. **Time Constraints**: If you need videos generated quickly, Vidu Turbo is the better choice.

Each model supports the same API interface, making it easy to switch between models based on your needs.
